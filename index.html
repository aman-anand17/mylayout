    Netaji Subhash Engineering College

    Netaji Subhash Engineering College (NSEC) has been set up, keeping in mind the ideals of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose, whose contribution to the Indian Freedom Movement remains invaluable forever.

    Netaji had dreamt of vibrant, strong and powerful India, an India which would be independent and self reliant, not only politically, but also in the spheres of Education & Technology. He strongly believed that true independence can only stem from strong base in education and technology. His vision is our beacon and to give a concrete shape to his vision, we have set up Netaji Subhash Engineering College.

    We believe in his ideals that intelligent minds are the greatest resources in any walk of life. Our vision is to infuse young, intelligent minds with cutting edge technical education and help them to become mature professionals poised and ready to accept technical and managerial challenges in the global

    CSE

    In today's Industrial Environment, the disciplines of Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology have come to the forefront for providing tools and techniques to the rest of industry - Heavy Engineering, Science Sector, Finance Sector, Entertainment Sector and all other basic Industries. Especially after the advent of Internet the requirement for the design of newer and newer Computer applications has increased million fold, as even the small traders and businessmen have become globally connected and e-commerce has become the new business model.

    The Department has the following laboratories:

    • DBMS Laboratory
    • Programming Laboratory I
    • Programming Laboratory II
    • Object Technology Laboratory
    • Network Laboratory
    • Research Laboratory
    • OS Laboratory
    • Microprocessor & Micro-controller Laboratory
    • CSE Project Laboratory for carrying out final year Projects
    • M.Tech. Laboratory

    The Faculty of the department is highly qualified and the focus areas of research that is carried out in the department are

    • Bio Informatics
    • Cloud computing
    • Machine Learning & Big data
    • Web Technology
    • Cryptography
